What is Game Design?

Game development is the processes of creating video games but many don't know all the aspects that go into it. Programming is one of these with lines of code and text a person can make a world come to live. Art can be expressed lively and so creatively with different art styles and in 2-D or 3-D art takes the breath away from players.
